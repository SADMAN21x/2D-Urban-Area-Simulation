## Introduction
This project is an interactive multi-scene simulation created using OpenGL (GLUT) and C++. It combines three different environments in one program: the Dhaka MRT-6 Metro Rail, a rural road with traffic signals, and an amusement park with animated objects. Users can switch between scenes by pressing the X key.
The project demonstrates important Computer Graphics concepts such as 2D transformations, animation, scene switching, color effects, and drawing using OpenGL primitives.

## Output Description
Scene 1 — Rural Road Traffic Output
This scene tries to represent a rural city environment. In the upper part of the screen, there are houses, tall buildings, distant skyscrapers, and a hospital. In front of the buildings, there are lamp posts and a tree. In the sky, there is an airplane and five flying birds with animated wings. During the daytime, a white cloud can be seen in the sky. The sun slowly rises and sets. At night, two lamp posts spread light on the road, stars twinkle beautifully in the sky, and the moon appears. The dark night sky and lamp post lights make the city road look realistic.
In the lower half of the screen, a road is shown where five human characters are walking around on the footpath. On the main road, six cars move automatically. There are two lanes: the upper lane moves from left to right, and the lower lane moves from right to left. Each lane contains three cars, and they always stay in their own lane. When one car comes too close behind another car, it slows down and follows the front car. At that time, the backlights of the cars glow to show that they are moving slowly in traffic.
There is also a zebra crossing and a traffic light system with red, yellow, and green lights. When the red light turns on, the cars stop before the zebra crossing and do not cross it. Cars on the other parts of the road continue moving normally. When the green light turns on, the cars cross the zebra crossing and continue driving. During the yellow light, the cars move slowly while crossing that area.
At night, the headlights of the cars spread light on the road in front of them. One human character crosses the road only when the traffic light is red. During green and yellow lights, the person does not cross the road. When the red light turns on again, the person returns to the previous side of the road. The other people continue walking on the footpath.
This scene also includes a rain effect. The speed of the rain can be increased or decreased gradually.
Scene 2 — Amusement Park Output
In this scene, a green field is shown with ten trees of different shapes and sizes. Some of the trees contain colorful flowers such as white, pink, red, and purple flowers. White, red, and pink flowers are also scattered around the green field, making the park look beautiful and natural.
There are two benches in the park. Each bench is divided into two parts. The upper part looks like a backrest for leaning, while the lower part is used for sitting. The legs of the benches are also visible below them. Around some trees, grass, and small stones can be seen, which adds more realistic detail to the environment.
A curved pathway is shown in the middle of the park, which looks like a soil or dirt road. There is also a Ferris wheel in the park that rotates continuously. The Ferris wheel contains eight colorful passenger cabins for riding.
At the far end of the park, a fence surrounds the area. In the sky, clouds move slowly while the sun remains fixed during the daytime. At night, the sky becomes dark, and stars twinkle beautifully.
There are also three human characters in the park. They move freely around the field and walk in different areas of the park on their own.
Scene 3 — Metro Rail Town Output
This scene represents a modern metro city environment. In the background, there are eleven tall buildings. Some red lights are placed on the rooftops of the buildings, which glow at night. During nighttime, some windows of the buildings light up while others remain dark, creating a realistic city atmosphere.
In the sky, several clouds can be seen. During the daytime, the sun spreads light across the sky, while at night the moon appears and lights up the dark sky. Numerous stars twinkle beautifully during the night.
Below the buildings, a metro rail line and a metro station are shown. Two metro trains move on the railway track. Each metro contains five compartments. One metro train has “MOTIJHEEL” written on the front window and moves from left to right. Another metro train has “UTTARA N” written on the front and moves from right to left on the same railway track. In the middle of the station, the text “MOTIJHEEL MRT 6” is displayed.
The speed of the metro can be increased or decreased. The minimum speed is zero, and the maximum speed is 16 kilometers per hour. The current speed is displayed on the screen, and the whole system can be controlled using the keyboard.
Under the metro rail, several shops are placed beside the road. The shops are labeled FOOD, BOOKS, FRUIT, VEG, CLOTH, PHONE, SPICE, and TOYS. Each shop has a different color. In front of the shops, small trees can be seen, with two different types of trees placed around the area. There are also seven lamp posts that spread light on the road at night. The shops also emit light in front of them at night.
The road is divided into two lanes where many colorful cars move continuously. At night, the cars spread light from their front headlights. Vehicles in both lanes move in opposite directions, from left to right and right to left. At the end of the screen, several small trees are placed beside the road. A total of six trees with two different designs are shown in that area.

## Keyboard Function
Press 1, 2, 3 to change the scenario.
Scene 1, 't' and 'y' used to start and pause the scene. In the traffic light system, 'r' for red, 'g' for green, 'n' for yellow light. 'f' and 'c' for rain start and stop, 'w' and 's' for rain drops fast and slow.
Scene 2, 'v' used for shifting day to night, again night to day with the same key.
Scene 3, all keywords have been written in the scenario.
